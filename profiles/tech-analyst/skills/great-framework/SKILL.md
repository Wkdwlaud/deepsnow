# GREAT Five-Dimension Analysis Framework

Score each dimension 1-10. Provide specific evidence for each score.

## G — Growth (成长性)

Measures current growth speed AND future 3-5 year trajectory.

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 9-10 | Revenue/profit growing >50% with clear 3+ year visibility |
| 7-8 | Growing 30-50%, strong product pipeline |
| 5-6 | Growing 15-30%, moderate visibility |
| 3-4 | Growing <15% or decelerating significantly |
| 1-2 | Flat or declining |

Key questions: What drives growth? Is it sustainable? Acceleration or deceleration?

## R — Replicable (可复制性)

Can the business model scale? Can success in one product/market replicate to others?

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 9-10 | Platform model, each new user/product adds marginal value at near-zero cost |
| 7-8 | Strong replication track record (geographic expansion, product line extension) |
| 5-6 | Moderate replication potential, requires significant investment |
| 3-4 | Limited replication, highly customized or local |
| 1-2 | One-trick pony, no replication path |

## E — Potential/Space (空间天花板)

How big can this company become? 吴伟志: "至少5倍空间才值得重仓。"

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 9-10 | 10x+ potential from current market cap, massive TAM |
| 7-8 | 5-10x potential, clear path to much larger scale |
| 5-6 | 3-5x potential |
| 3-4 | <3x potential, approaching ceiling |
| 1-2 | At or near ceiling |

Calculate using Future Market Cap Method: TAM × Expected Share × Margin × Fair PE

## A — Excellent Management (管理层)

Values, vision, capability, track record, culture, incentive alignment.

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 9-10 | Visionary founder, proven track record, strong culture, skin in game |
| 7-8 | Competent team, good capital allocation history |
| 5-6 | Adequate management, no red flags |
| 3-4 | Concerns about governance, capital allocation, or alignment |
| 1-2 | Serious governance issues or value-destructive behavior |

## T — Return Certainty (业绩确定性)

How confident are we that projected results will actually materialize?

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 9-10 | Near-certain delivery: strong backlog, recurring revenue, proven execution |
| 7-8 | High confidence, multiple catalysts with clear timelines |
| 5-6 | Moderate confidence, depends on 1-2 key variables |
| 3-4 | Low confidence, multiple uncertainties |
| 1-2 | Highly speculative, binary outcome |

## Scoring Guide

- **40-50/50**: Exceptional — Core pool candidate (must be "Tree" type)
- **35-39/50**: Strong — Watch pool, potential satellite position
- **25-34/50**: Average — Reserve pool only
- **<25/50**: Weak — Do not track
